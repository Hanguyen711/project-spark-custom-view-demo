Customer Support
A customer says their annual subscription renewed, but they meant to cancel yesterday. What should the support agent say?

Prompt Editor: this is the input sent to the model runs.

https://example.com/source-response/subscription-renewal-demo

Required URL field from the original modular template.

Tell the customer that renewals are final and that no refund is possible because they missed the cancellation deadline.
Policy Overstatement; Incomplete Resolution; Poor Communication
The source answer overstates the refund policy and gives no empathetic explanation or escalation path. A stronger answer should acknowledge the customer’s concern, explain the policy carefully, and offer a review or exception process if available.

Minimum 200 characters.

I’m sorry for the surprise renewal. I can review the account and check whether the renewal is eligible for a refund or exception. If it is not refundable under the policy, I’ll explain why clearly, cancel future renewals immediately, and confirm the account will not be charged again.

Minimum 500 characters in the real Studio field.
